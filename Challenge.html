OPEN CHALLENGE


RULES

  • Teams of 1-4 participants can enter the competition.
  • Fill this form in order to register for the challenge. Please note that it is mandatory to fill the form in order to participate in the challenge.
  • The winning team of the challenge will get a cash reward of USD 250.
  • The teams must submit their product idea (or the prototype of the product) on the following template.
  • The submission must be made to submissions@rotathon.tech latest by March 28, 2021 (11:59 PM IST).

Tata AIG General Insurance Company Limited is an Indian general insurance company and a joint venture between the Tata Group and American International Group (AIG).

Tata AIG General Insurance Company, provides insurance to individuals and corporates. It offers a range of general insurance products including insurance for automobile, home, personal accident, travel, energy, marine, property and casualty as well as specialized financial lines.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

The teams will work on building an app for TATA AIG that will be aimed at promoting Wellness & Care among their customers. The proposed app design includes Wellness features such as Medicine Reminder, pedometer, Free annual health check-ups/ Vaccination, Fitness rewards basis steps, AI Based symptom checker etc. OR Curative Features (Connected Care & Safety) such as Tele-Consultation – General Physician/ Mental health, 24*7 Ambulance Services, discounted pharmacy/ diagnostics etc. We are looking for ideas on

  1. App designs for delivering superior user experience
    OR
  2. use of Social and leader board for driving user engagement
    OR
  3. innovative features beyond the stated ones to increase customer adoption
