WHAT IS ROTATHON?

ROTATHON, Hackathon & Ideathon, invites social entrepreneurs and innovators across the globe to develop solutions for a spectrum of problem statements. After the screening of ideas in phase 1, the top 30 teams will be shortlisted. These teams will go through a round of rigorous training and consecutive mentorship to bring their ideas to life. The teams must prepare for a never seen before mega-challenging showdown in April!

WHY JOIN US?

  • </>Rotathon inculcates social entrepreneurship and responsibility
  • </>It is a hub of curious innovators from all age groups and professions
  • </>It is a source of business models and huge social and environmental impact

SCHEDULE

Registration Phase 1

January 21, 2021 to February 15, 2021
In this phase, the team will be given the Problem Statements for each Track. The teams need to choose their desired track and register for Hackathon or Ideathon

Registration Phase 2

February 15, 2021 to March 3, 2021
By the end of the phase, the teams need to provide a solution to the given problem statement. Each registration will be considered complete after the submission of PPTs on the shared template

Screening Round 1

March 4, 2021 to March 13, 2021
During this phase, mentorship sessions for all the teams will be conducted. The Top 30 Teams will be shortlisted.

Opening Ceremony

March 18, 2021
The Opening Ceremony will mark the opening of the final stages which will consist of the selected 30 Teams.

Mentorship and Training

March 18, 2021 to April 3, 2021
The top 30 teams will receive mentorship and guidance from field experts so that they can refine their product and pitch

Rotathon Grand Finale

April 2021
Final Screening of pitches of Hackathon and Ideathon to declare the ultimate winners of Rotathon!
