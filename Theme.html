Rotathon brings this theme alive by deploying the process of effective solutions to systematic issues in support of social progress leads to entrepreneurship. The Rotathon community builds this fraternity to connect changemakers from around the world to embark on this enterprise of innovation.


VISION

Rotaract’s vision of Social Change and the challenge of hackathon (and Ideathon) combined is brought your way by ROTATHON!

The first of its kind, Rotathon aims to reach out to the vast international Rotaract fraternity in order to work hand in hand and bring together changemakers from across the world. Rotathon envisions a future built collectively by the citizens of the world who don’t just see a problem, but an opportunity.

Rotathon is your one-stop destination where ideas take flight and innovation breeds invention!
