About Us

ABOUT ROTARACT

Rotary International is a non - profit International service Organisation that was found with the vision of one man - Paul Harris back in 1905. Rotary brings together the Professionals and the Business Leaders from diverse fields to provide Humanitarian Services and form Lifelong Friendships. Currently, there are around 1.2 million Rotarians working across the globe for the Rotary movement. Rotaract is the youth wing of Rotary International that works in many Service Avenues - Community Services, International Services, Professional Development etc. with the motto ‘Self Development: Fellowship through Service’, 203,298 Rotaractors are spread across 108 Countries to engage in Personal Development through their service to the Community. Rotaract and Rotary together work for the causes like Ending Polio, Supporting Education, Disaster Response, Providing Clean Water and other such causes.

ABOUT ROTARACT DISTRICT - 3011 (RID-3011)

Rotaract District 3011 is one district recognised by Rotary International, that looks over 55 + Rotaract Clubs spread across Delhi and NCR. Overall, we have over 2500+ Rotaractors working for the Rotaract Movement with projects like Mass Plantation Drives, Youth Cultural Exchange Programs, Professional Development sessions, Mental Well-Being Projects and much more, in collaboration with similar Rotaract Districts from the country and beyond. The District has been nurturing young leaders tirelessly since 2015 to promote fellowship, better international relations and above all, community welfare.
