MESSAGE FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL


Dear All,

I am Joshika Saraf and it is an absolute honor for me to welcome each one of you to the 2018 Special Session of Rotaract Global Model United Nations Conference (RGMUN) in Delhi, India to be held from 20th April 2018 to 22nd April 2018. My association with RGMUN goes back to the sessions that were held in Belgrade, Serbia (2015) and the United Nations Headquarters, New York City (2016) where I was given the opportunity to chair the UNGA-Legal and the UNHCR respectively.

Keeping in consideration, all the previous RGMUN editions in Baia Mare - Romania, London - United Kingdom, Belgrade - Serbia, the UN Headquarters - New York, Sofia –Bulgaria and Beirut-Lebanon, it is correct to say that Rotaract is indeed felicitous for the organization of a successful international conference that offers substantially sound debate as well deliberation, which inturn leads to effective decisions. RGMUN endorses the preparation of all participants to attain knowledge and high level skills that can be put to good use in the future, including possession of instruments for a better understanding of global dynamics and their elucidation, and the ability to relate and further manage affiliations with the globalized world in which we live, in terms of cultures, political opinions, and value systems other than their own.

Bearing in mind Prof. Jeffrey D. Sachs’ idea about the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), the theme for this year’s conference is “Sustainable Development Goals: Fostering a brighter future.” I firmly believe that the SDGs will give us a chance to eradicate the evil of poverty and to ensure that the basic human rights are well respected. The agendas/topic areas to be discussed in this very special session resonate with the idea mentioned above and I look forward to some remarkable discussions in the committee sessions.

To conclude, I must admit that I am waiting to host you all with my entire team at the City of Djinns with all eagerness, for experiencing a symbiotic relationship like never before.
Till then,
May the odds be ever in your favor!

Joshika Saraf
Secretary General
Rotaract Global MUN Special Edition 2018

MESSAGE FROM THE PRESIDENT OF ROTARY CLUB OF DELHI REGENCY


Greetings from Rotary Club of Delhi Regency!

We have always lived by the motto of Rotary, that is, "Service above Self". Rotary Year 2017-18 has been a special year indeed. We have laid a strong foundation in community services, Free Eye Cataract Operations are done every month at Venus Eye Hospital for the underprivileged (Sponsored by the club). Successful Blood Donation camps have also been carried out.

This year has bought many feathers in our cap. It was an honour to be awarded for Maximum contribution & participation in Rotary Foundation Ball of RID 3011. The club has also been awarded by RI President (2014-15) Gary C.K. Huang on becoming a major donor.

I am extremely honoured and humbled that Rotaract Club of DTU Regency has been awarded the honour to organise Rotaract Global Model United Nations Special Edition 2018 in April 2018. RaCDTU has always been consistent in terms of performance, always organising events with enthusiasm and zeal. We extend our full support to our Rotaract Club to organise a successful event. We look forward to welcome you all to this remarkable ride where Debate and Indian Culture provides you with beautiful memories to cherish.

Rtn. Rajender Singhal
President
Rotary Club of Delhi Regency

MESSAGE FROM THE PRESIDENT OF ROTARACT CLUB OF DTU REGENCY


Greetings to all!

I am extremely honoured and delighted to host delegates from all around the globe at RGMUN 2018 Special Edition.
I have been been associated with Rotaract since my freshman year. Over the years, I've watched RaCDTU grow immensely.
